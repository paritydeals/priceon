Frequently asked questions

Do I need to enter credit card information to signup or try?

No credit card information is required to get started.

How do you determine the number of hits?

Only the number of hits from the targeted pages (usually the pricing page) are considered hits. You won't be charged for the regular page visits.

What happens if I exceed the number of hits in my current plan?

We'll never charge you for the occasional traffic spikes, and our service will work as usual. But, if you regularly cross the permitted hits, we'll contact you and ask you to upgrade your account. There are no hidden charges!

How do I cancel my paid plan?

You can cancel your paid subscription anytime. To switch to the BASIC plan, visit the subscriptions page.
