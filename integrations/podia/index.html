Easily link your accounts and configure location-based pricing and promotions for your products with just a few clicks.

How to integrate Podia with ParityDeals

Create deals

Start adding your product by selecting ‘Create manually’ from the ‘Create deals’ dropdown.

Select deal types and setup product info

Please note that you need to enter the exact website URL where you want to display the banner with the coupon code.

Verify pricing rules

Verify the discount percentage for each country group. You need to create coupon codes on your Podia account and add them here.

Go to your Podia coupons section and create coupons

Paste the same coupon code that you have created on ParityDeals on your Podia coupons section to make sure two-way sync.

Customize your banner

Now configure the banner that will be added to your site. You could adjust the text, design, and placement of the banner as per your website theme.

Add the script tag

Add the following script tag to your Podia account.

<script src="https://cdn.paritydeals.com/banner.js"></script>
documentation for adding banner to Podia account.

Banner preview

After adding ParityDeals to your Podia account, targeted customers from different countries will see a banner on your page. The banner includes a discount code that corresponds to their location, allowing them to enjoy a reduced price at checkout.

To test the banner, add a temporary discount coupon for your own country. Please note that VPN usage is detected and prevented from displaying coupons.
